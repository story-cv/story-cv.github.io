Why the industry has it backwards

Resume writing became a game of keywords.

Somewhere along the way, we stopped writing for people. We started optimizing for algorithms.
And in the process, we forgot the real point of a resume: to tell your story and open doors.

Most people struggle to explain what they actually did.
Understandably so. Not because they lack impact, but because putting your journey into words is hard. You either undersell, overinflate or miss the magic completely.

We couldn't find a single tool that helped you build a strong foundation and not just the polish bullet points in your resume.

So we built StoryCV: A new kind of resume writer that listens, guides and helps you uncover the value you bring.

Because you shouldn't have to send out 100 applications and hope.

A great resume should open doors with just a few.
