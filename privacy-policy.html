Effective Date: August 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how StoryCV ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

When you sign up for an account, we collect your name and email address. If you sign up using a social login (Google, LinkedIn), we access the information you permit through those platforms.

User Inputs

This includes resume content, chat interactions, and voice inputs you provide.

Payment Information

Payments are processed through Stripe. We do not store your full payment details.

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data to improve our services.

2. Use of Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To personalize your experience
  • To communicate with you
  • To process transactions and apply parity pricing
  • To analyze usage trends and improve functionality

3. Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on servers hosted by Digital Ocean. We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Sharing of Information

We only share your information with third parties in the following cases:

  • With service providers (Google, Stripe, Parity Deals, Digital Ocean) that assist in operating StoryCV
  • As required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccuracies
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at write@story.cv

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Where required by law, we display a cookie banner to obtain consent.

7. Children's Privacy

StoryCV is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by revising the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, email us at write@story.cv
